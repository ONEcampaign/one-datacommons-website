Report Data Issues

Encountered an error or something unusual in our data? Your feedback is invaluable in helping us maintain accuracy and quality. Here's how you can alert us:

  1. Email Us: Reach out directly with a detailed description of the issue.
  2. GitHub Issue: Prefer a more technical route? File an issue on our GitHub repository at https://github.com/ONEcampaign/one-datacommons-website/

To ensure a swift and effective response, please include:

  • A clear description of the problem.
  • The specific URL where the issue was noticed.
  • A screenshot for visual reference, if possible.

Your contributions play a crucial role in improving Data Commons for everyone. Thank you for your support and attention to detail!
